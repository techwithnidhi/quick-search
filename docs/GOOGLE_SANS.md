# Google Sans in Quick Search

Quick Search's `standard` distribution bundles Google Sans font files.

As of November 2025, Google Fonts states that Google Sans and Google Sans Flex are available under the SIL Open Font License:

- https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq#can_i_use_the_google_sans_and_google_sans_flex_fonts

For F-Droid, the app does not bundle these font files. The `fdroid` variant uses the system font instead so the artifact stays independent of bundled branded font assets.
